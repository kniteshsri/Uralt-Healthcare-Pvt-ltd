About Uralt Healthcare

Founded in Feb 2024, Uralt Healthcare is a fast-growing pharmaceutical company dedicated to delivering high-quality, affordable healthcare products.

We specialize in the marketing and distribution of a wide range of pharmaceutical formulations, manufactured through trusted third-party partnerships.

We have built a strong presence across key markets, offering innovative therapies backed by quality and compliance.

Headquartered in Jaipur, India. We are committed to providing high-quality pharmaceutical products that meet global standards while ensuring accessibility and affordability for all.

Our Mission

  • To ensure access to high-quality medicines through strategic third-party manufacturing partnerships.
  • To offer a diverse and effective range of healthcare solutions to patients and healthcare professionals.
  • To establish a strong national presence built on trust, quality, and service excellence.
  • Foster innovation through continuous research and development.
  • Build lasting relationships with healthcare professionals and stakeholders.
  • Ensure ethical practices in all aspects of our business operations.

Our Core Values

Uralt Healthcares products are manufactured in WHO-GMP-certified facilities ensuring safety and efficacy.
We uphold ethical standards in all our business dealings.
Uralt is a customer focused company, and we understand and meet the needs of healthcare providers and patients.
We are dedicated in introducing advanced formulations and therapies in line with the latest scientific developments.

  • Quality: We uphold the highest standards in every product we deliver.
  • Innovation: We constantly explore and develop advanced healthcare solutions.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and responsibility.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to making healthcare affordable and available for all.
